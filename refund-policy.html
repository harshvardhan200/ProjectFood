Refund Policy

Last updated: December 11,2024.

We strive to ensure you are completely satisfied with our services. If you are not entirely satisfied with your purchase, we're here to help.

Refunds

If you are not satisfied with the quality of the food or the service provided, you can request a refund within 24 hours of receiving your order. To be eligible for a refund, please contact our customer support with your order details and a description of the issue.

Eligibility for Refunds

  • Food quality issues: If the food delivered is not up to standard (e.g., spoiled, wrong order).
  • Order errors: If there was an error in the order due to our mistake (e.g., missing items).
  • Late deliveries: If the delivery is significantly delayed beyond the promised time.

Process for Refunds

  • Contact customer support via email or phone within 3 hours of receiving your order.
  • Provide your order number and a description of the issue.
  • Our team will review your request and process the refund if eligible.
  • Refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days and will be credited to your original method of payment.
